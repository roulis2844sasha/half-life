Sienna - Bomb/Defuse

Background: The town of Sienna is under
high alert following new information
gathered by the intelligence services.
Access in and out of the town has been 
blocked to prevent a terrorist attack.

Terrorists: Identify the two vulnerable
points of entry and gain access by 
detonating explosives near them.

Counter-Terrorists:  Prevent the
Terrorists from destroying the gates
and entering the town.

Other Notes: There are 2 entrances 
the Terrorists may choose to attack.
